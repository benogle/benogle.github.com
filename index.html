startupga.me

About

Startupga.me is an experiment in response to an observation we made recently while attending numerous meetups and events here in the valley. There are a lot of people working on interesting projects and there are a lot of people looking to work on interesting projects. However, it seems to us that efforts made from either camp to find folks from the other are very inefficient. We want to help.

What we will do

Our role will be to sift through the responses we get, identify potential matches in interest and ability and eventually make introductions. For a number of reasons, we've decided this is not going to be a site where you post and respond to posts. Based on our experience, we don't feel websites are the most effective approach.

From the feedback we get, we may take a number of approaches depending on the type and volume of responses. This may include anything from setting up one-on-one meetings to hosting events where we bring participants with similar interests together in an informal event to meet one another, to possibly setting up a co-working space where participants can share collaborate on their projects.

This experiment is not for recruiting employees unless those employees are #1 and #2. We are interested in helping people get projects off the ground.

If you'd like to be a part of this, please fill out our form. And we'd love to get your feedback on what we're doing!

Questions or feedback can be sent to Joann Kuo or Ben Ogle.
